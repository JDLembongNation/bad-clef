About me

- Intricate whilst Inventive. -

Intricate - ˈɪntrɪkət
adjective - very complicated or detailed.

Inventive - ɪnˈvɛntɪv
adjective - having the ability to create or design new things or to think originally.

My name is Joshua Domenico Lembong, considering myself as what most millenials and culturally sensitive people would describe as "third cultured". Throughout my life, I've lived in Singapore, Hong Kong, London, and Jakarta. Each of those places have added a certain unique touch to my personality and have crafted a new perspective towards my overall thought process. I'm not saying that this is a good thing, I've had my plenty of shares of cultural issues and offensive behaviour (which can be read about on my blog) but I am able to also strongly connect with many people from all over the world.
